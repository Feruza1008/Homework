POWERS

Square Numbers - A square is obtained by multiplying a number by itself.

e.g. 5 2= 5 x 5=25

Cubic numbers - A cubic number is obtained by multyplying a number by itself twice.

e.g. 5 3= 5 x 5 x 5=125

Powers - A power tells you the number of times you multyply the number together.

e.g. 2 4= 2 x 2 x 2 x 2=16 (Multiple four 2s together)
